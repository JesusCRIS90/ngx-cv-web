Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit this website and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

When you visit this website, we may collect limited technical and usage information for the sole purpose of improving the performance, usability, and reliability of the site.

  • Browser type (e.g. Chrome, Firefox)
  • Device type (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop)
  • Client Resolution Tags (big-screen, medium-screen, etc)
  • Operating system / platform
  • Preferred language
  • Approximate location (country, region, city)
  • Visit time rounded to coarse intervals (hour or 15-minute blocks)

This information is collected in an aggregated and minimized form and does not directly identify you.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No IP addresses are stored
  • No cookies or tracking cookies are used
  • No device fingerprinting is performed
  • No persistent identifiers are created
  • No personal identifiers such as name, email, or account data

3. Purpose of Processing

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Understanding general usage patterns
  • Optimizing layout and responsiveness
  • Improving performance and user experience
  • Detecting technical issues

The data is not used for advertising, profiling, or cross-site tracking.

4. Legal Basis

The processing of this limited technical data is based on our legitimate interest in maintaining and improving the website, in accordance with Article 6(1)(f) of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

5. Data Retention

Collected analytics data is retained for a limited period and automatically deleted after it is no longer necessary for the purposes described above.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer collected analytics data to third parties.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you have any questions regarding this policy or the processing of data, you may contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or website functionality.
