Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit this website, how it is processed, and for what purposes.

1. Data We Collect

When you visit this website, we collect limited technical and usage information strictly necessary to improve performance, usability, and reliability.

  • Browser type (e.g. Chrome, Firefox)
  • Device type (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop)
  • Client resolution category (e.g. big-screen, medium-screen)
  • Operating system / platform
  • Preferred language
  • Approximate location (country, region, city)
  • Visit time (rounded to coarse intervals such as 15-minute blocks)

Approximate location is derived from the visitor’s IP address at request time. The IP address is used transiently for this purpose and is not stored.

This information is collected in a minimized form and stored without direct identifiers. It is not used to identify individual visitors.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No IP addresses are stored
  • No cookies or tracking cookies are used
  • No device fingerprinting is performed
  • No persistent identifiers are created
  • No personal identifiers such as name, email, or account data

3. Purpose of Processing

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Understanding general usage patterns
  • Optimizing layout and responsiveness
  • Improving performance and user experience
  • Detecting and diagnosing technical issues

The data is not used for advertising, profiling, behavioral tracking, or cross-site tracking. It is not combined to create user profiles or to uniquely identify visitors.

4. Legal Basis

The processing of this limited technical data is based on our legitimate interest in maintaining and improving the website, in accordance with Article 6(1)(f) of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

We ensure that this processing is limited, proportionate, and does not override the fundamental rights and freedoms of visitors.

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for a maximum period of 90 days and is automatically deleted thereafter.

6. Data Sharing and Processing

We do not sell or share analytics data with third parties.

Data is processed and stored using infrastructure providers acting as data processors under our instructions (e.g. hosting and database services).
